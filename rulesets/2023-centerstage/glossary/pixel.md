# Pixel

A hexagonal shaped |Scoring Element|, 3-inches (76.2 mm) across by 0.5-inches
(12.7 mm) thick. There are sixty-four (64) white Pixels, ten (10) yellow Pixels,
ten (10) green Pixels, and ten (10) purple Pixels.
