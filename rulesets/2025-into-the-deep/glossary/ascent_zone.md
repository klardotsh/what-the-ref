# Ascent Zone

an infinitely tall 5-sided polygon that is formed from two 9.25 in. (\~23.5 cm)
long sides bounded by the [SUBMERSIBLE](!!) outriggers, one 44.75 in. (\~113.7
cm) long side bounded by the barrier of the [SUBMERSIBLE](!!), and the two 26
in. (\~66 cm) long sides bounded by white tape that extend from the outriggers
to a point 20 in. (~50.8 cm) from the barrier. The ASCENT ZONE includes the
taped lines.
